Bio
Izel Maras is a designer and a conceptual artist who investigates the modes of experience created via internet and new media. Her projects document and reflect on the normalization of technology and attempts to ridicule the loopholes of the world of tomorrow. Izel Maras has studied german literature, visual arts and computer science at the school for arts and sciences in Tufts University. She completed a bachelor's degree in International Literary and Visual Arts with a special focus on new media arts. Since 2014 she has been working with Marcelo Coelho at his studio in Cambridge. Marcelo Coelho is an artist, designer and researcher, whose work explores how physical and computational materials can be used to create new human experiences. The strong connection to the Digitality also influences her Art. She is an experienced graphic designer, a periodic website developer and a decent programmer. Yet she finds pleasure in building singlemind websites, making Gifs, envisioning large-scale performances and installations,and enthusiasing about media hacking. She is from Turkey, currently residing in Boston.
