Explore the streets with a different view

Pedlar is a mobile application where you can find what you are looking for in a nearby street, ranging from food to clothing or art.

A Better Search
Experience

Users are allowed to search through the map, using wide range of filters.

Wide Variety of Products

Anything that can be sold on the streets is included in the filters.

Real-Time Tracking

Street vendors are tracked real time for users to find at the exact location.

Reliable Reviews

Users are allowed to comment about anything they buy or try on the vendor’s store page.
