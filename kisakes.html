Cut Yo’ Film Short!

  • Translating ”to cut short” in Turkish, Kisakes is a film festival based in Istanbul. For them I’ve designed a bold and playful visual identity based on  scissors and glasses symbols.

    The most challenging part of the project was to design an identity that would allow brand expansion, for the organization grew rapidly within past 4 years. Anchoring the brand image to two main symbols, scissors and glasses, I biannually redesigned an identity for Kisakes film festivals.
